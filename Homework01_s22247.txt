The scene contains:
player object,
platforms,
object to reset the scene.

Player features:
walks,
runs (with shift),
jumps,
double jumps,
dashes (with Ctrl).

Platform features
a platform disappearing 1s after collision with player and reappearing after one more second,
a platform that moves left/right - the player moves together with the platform,
a platform that is a trampoline,
a random platform (when player collides with it it randomly chooses what type to be: moving or disapppearing completely i.e. troll platform).
